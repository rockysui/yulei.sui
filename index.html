Bio

I am a Lecturer and an ARC DECRA fellow working at Faculty of Engineering and Information Technology , University of Technology Sydney (UTS) . I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of New South Wales, Sydney, where I was advised by Sciential Professor Jingling Xue.

I am broadly interested in Software Engineering and Programming Languages. In particularly, my research focuses on building fundamental static and dynamic program analysis techniques and tools to improve the reliability and security of modern software systems. My recent interest lies in bug prediction, program repair and software security analysis through data mining and machine learning.

I'm looking for motivated students who are interested in my research with background in software engineering, machine learning or security. Full PhD Scholarships with tuition fee wave will be provided to qualified candidates. Please feel free to contact me (ysui@cse.unsw.edu.au)!
